Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Overview

When your check engine light comes on or your car isn't performing as it should, our advanced diagnostic tools quickly identify the problem. From sensor replacements to major engine repairs, we provide expert solutions to restore performance and reliability.

What's included

  • Accurate problem identification
  • Efficient repairs save time and money
  • Improved fuel economy and performance
  • Comprehensive engine health check

How it works

1

Computer Scan

We connect to your vehicle's computer to read error codes and live data.

2

Diagnostic Testing

We perform additional tests (compression, vacuum, fuel pressure) to confirm the issue.

3

Repair & Verification

We complete the repair and clear codes, then test drive to verify the fix.
