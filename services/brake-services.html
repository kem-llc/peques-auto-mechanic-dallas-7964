Brake Services

Complete brake inspection, repair, and replacement for safe stopping power.

Schedule this service

Overview

Your brakes are your vehicle's most critical safety system. At Peque's, we inspect pads, rotors, calipers, and brake lines to ensure optimal performance. Whether you're hearing squeaks or feeling vibrations, we'll diagnose the problem and recommend the right solution. We use high-quality parts to restore your braking system to factory standards.

What's included

  • Thorough inspection of all brake components
  • Quality parts for long-lasting performance
  • Competitive pricing with no hidden fees

How it works

1

Visual & Road Test

We check brake pad thickness, rotor condition, and test for pedal feel and noise.

2

Component Assessment

We measure rotor runout, inspect calipers, and check brake fluid condition.

3

Repair & Final Check

We replace worn parts, bleed the system if needed, and perform a final road test.

Common questions

How often should I get my brakes checked?

We recommend a brake inspection every 10,000 miles or once a year, whichever comes first.

Do you offer brake pad replacement only?

Yes, but we always recommend inspecting rotors as well. We'll advise you on the best option for your vehicle.
