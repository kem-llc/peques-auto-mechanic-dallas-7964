Overview

A malfunctioning AC or heater can make driving miserable. We service all makes and models, from refrigerant recharge to compressor replacement. Our technicians ensure your climate control system works efficiently, keeping you cool in summer and warm in winter.

What's included

  • Reliable cooling and heating
  • Improved air quality with cabin filter replacement
  • Prevents costly breakdowns
  • Eco-friendly refrigerant handling

How it works

1

System Inspection

We check refrigerant levels, compressor, condenser, and all components.

2

Leak Detection & Repair

We use UV dye and electronic detectors to find and fix leaks.

3

Recharge & Performance Test

We recharge to spec and test vent temperatures to ensure proper operation.

Common questions

How often should I recharge my AC?

Only when performance drops. If you need frequent recharges, there's likely a leak.

Can you fix a heater that blows cold air?

Yes – common causes include low coolant, a bad thermostat, or a faulty heater core.
