Overview

Your brakes are your vehicle's most critical safety system. At Peque's, we inspect, repair, and replace brake pads, rotors, calipers, and lines. We use quality components and provide honest recommendations – no unnecessary upsells.

What's included

  • Improved stopping power and safety
  • Reduced noise and vibration
  • Extended brake system life
  • Peace of mind with a warranty

How it works

1

Inspection

We thoroughly inspect pads, rotors, calipers, fluid, and lines.

2

We test your brakes at various speeds to ensure optimal performance.
