Our story

Your Neighborhood Auto Repair Experts

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair in Dallas, TX, building lasting relationships through transparency and craftsmanship.

Peque's Auto Mechanic was founded in 2005 with a simple mission: offer reliable auto repair without the upselling and hidden fees that plague the industry. Starting as a small two-bay shop on Forest Lane, we've grown into a trusted destination for Dallas drivers thanks to our commitment to quality and integrity.

Over the years, we've invested in the latest diagnostic equipment and ongoing training for our technicians. We treat every customer like a neighbor – because you are. Whether it's a simple oil change or a complex engine repair, we approach every job with the same dedication: do it right the first time.
