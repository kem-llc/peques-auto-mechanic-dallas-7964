Our story

Your Trusted Local Auto Mechanic Since 2010

Our mission is to provide honest, high-quality auto repair services that keep our customers safe and their vehicles running reliably. We believe in transparency, integrity, and building long-term relationships with the Dallas community.

Peque's Auto Mechanic was founded in 2010 with a simple vision: create a repair shop where customers feel respected and informed. Starting with a small garage on Forest Lane, owner Peque built a reputation for thorough diagnostics and fair pricing. Over the years, we've expanded our facility and team, but our core values remain unchanged.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across Dallas and surrounding areas. Our ASE-certified technicians continue to invest in training and equipment to stay ahead of the curve. We're proud to be a locally owned business that treats every car like it's our own.
